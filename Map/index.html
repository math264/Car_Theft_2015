Car Thefts in 2015 and the Most Targeted

Authored by: Michael A. Thomas @ New Maps Plus

Every year, a variety of make and models are reported in a grand theft auto; 2015 is no exception. The first map is designed to display all reported car thefts in 2015 nationally. The data displayed should come at no surprise, as population density can and often does affect crime rates. Nonetheless, the first map is only there to show the distribution of thefts in 2015.

The second map also displays national theft distribution, but categorically. Each state displays a vehicle that was targeted and stolen the most. Even though the most popular vehicle stolen from state-to-state were the older model Chevrolet Pickup trucks, it ranks fourth overall (27,771) in units stolen nationally. The top three were:

  • 1. Honda Accord (52,244 stolen)
  • 2. Honda Civiv (49,430 stolen)
  • 3. Ford Pickup (29,396 stolen
For more information on car thefts in 2015 or in any other year, please see the data sources listed in the metadata section!
